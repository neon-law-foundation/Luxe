About Neon Law Foundation

Founded in 2021 with a mission to protect privacy rights and promote digital security for everyone

Our Story

Founded: 2021

Neon Law Foundation was established in 2021 with a clear and urgent mission: to protect the fundamental privacy rights that every person deserves in our increasingly digital world. We recognized that as technology advances at breakneck speed, the rights of individuals to control their personal data and maintain their privacy were being eroded.

Our founders, passionate advocates for civil rights and digital privacy, saw the need for an organization that could bridge the gap between complex technology issues and the legal protections that individuals need. We believe that privacy is not a privilege for the few, but a fundamental right that belongs to everyone.

Since our founding, we have worked tirelessly to educate individuals and organizations about their privacy rights, provide resources for digital security, and advocate for fair treatment in our justice system. Our work is driven by three core beliefs:

  • Every person has the right to their own data - You should control what information about you is collected, how it's used, and who has access to it.
  • Every person has the right to be forgotten - You should have the ability to have your personal information deleted and removed from digital platforms when you choose.
  • Every person deserves fair treatment in our justice system - Your problems and concerns should be heard fairly, regardless of your background or circumstances.

Our team consists of passionate advocates, legal experts, and technology professionals who are dedicated to protecting privacy rights and promoting digital security. We bring together diverse expertise in law, technology, education, and advocacy to provide comprehensive support for our mission.
