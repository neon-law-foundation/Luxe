Request a Workshop

Our educational workshops are designed to empower individuals and organizations with the knowledge and skills needed to protect their privacy rights and enhance their digital security.

Available Workshop Topics

Privacy Rights Fundamentals

Understanding your data rights, GDPR/CCPA compliance, and privacy protection strategies

Digital Security Essentials

Cybersecurity best practices, secure communication tools, and threat protection

Civil Rights & Justice Access

Understanding your civil rights, legal resource navigation, and advocacy strategies

Organizational Privacy Programs

Implementing privacy programs, data governance, and compliance frameworks

Workshop Information

  • Duration: 1-4 hours (customizable)
  • Format: In-person or virtual
  • Audience: 10-100 participants
  • Cost: Free for non-profits and educational institutions
  • Materials: Included (guides, resources, certificates)
  • Follow-up: 30-day support included

Frequently Asked Questions

How quickly do you respond to inquiries?

We typically respond within 24-48 hours during business days. Urgent matters receive priority attention.

Do you charge for consultations?

Initial consultations and educational resources are provided free of charge. We believe privacy protection should be accessible to everyone.

Can you help with international privacy laws?

Yes, we provide guidance on GDPR, CCPA, and other international privacy regulations, as well as cross-border data protection issues.

Do you provide legal representation?

We provide legal guidance and resources, but do not serve as legal counsel. We can help connect you with appropriate legal representation when needed.

Are your workshops available virtually?

Yes, all our workshops are available in both in-person and virtual formats to accommodate different needs and preferences.

How can I support your mission?

You can support us through donations, volunteering, partnership opportunities, or by spreading awareness about privacy rights in your community.

Need Immediate Assistance?

If you're experiencing an urgent privacy violation, digital security threat, or civil rights issue that requires immediate attention, please contact us right away.
