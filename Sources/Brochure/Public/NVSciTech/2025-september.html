Discover Model Context Protocol (MCP) techniques for contract drafting, review, and analysis. Learn "safe vibe lawyering" principles that ensure AI assists without compromising professional judgment or creating liability risks.

What You'll Learn

Hallucination Prevention

Understand how to reduce hallucinations with summarization instead of asking the LLM to create its own results.

Model Context Protocol (MCP)

Learn Anthropic's open standard for connecting AI systems to data sources securely, enabling safer integration of Claude and other LLMs with legal databases and practice management systems.

Safe Vibe Lawyering Framework

Master the principle of asking LLMs to summarize rather than create, following strict verification rules where each AI output is validated against primary sources and professional judgment.

Sagebrush Standards

Learn the Sagebrush Standards for safe AI lawyering, which are specific instructions for how to write legal document swith markdown and YAML.
