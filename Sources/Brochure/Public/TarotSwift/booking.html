What to Include in Your Booking Email

To provide you with the most accurate and insightful reading, please include the following information in your booking email to team@tarotswift.me:

Essential Birth Information:

  • Birth Date: Month, day, and year of birth
  • Birth Time: Exact time (hour and minute) - check your birth certificate if possible
  • Birth Location: City, state/province, and country where you were born
  • Time Zone Awareness: If you know the time zone or daylight saving status, include it

Reading Preferences:

  • Reading Type: Birth chart reading, compatibility reading, or both
  • Session Format: In-person (if local) or virtual session preference
  • Time Preferences: Days and times that work best for your schedule
  • Urgency Level: If you have specific timing needs or are flexible

Your Questions & Focus Areas:

  • Specific Questions: Life areas you'd like to explore (career, relationships, life purpose, etc.)
  • Current Situation: Any major decisions or life transitions you're navigating
  • Experience Level: Your familiarity with astrology (beginner, intermediate, advanced)
  • Special Interests: Any particular astrological traditions you're most curious about

For Compatibility Readings:

  • Partner's Birth Data: Complete birth information for the other person (with their permission)
  • Relationship Type: Romantic, business, family, friendship, etc.
  • Relationship History: How long you've known each other, current status
  • Specific Relationship Questions: Areas of the relationship you'd like to understand better

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Do I need to know my exact birth time?

A: Yes, accurate birth time is essential for precise chart calculations in all three traditions. If you don't know it, check your birth certificate or contact the hospital where you were born. If absolutely unavailable, we can discuss alternative approaches.

Q: What if I'm skeptical about astrology?

A: Perfect! Healthy skepticism leads to better questions and more meaningful exploration. I focus on practical insights and patterns you can verify in your own life experience rather than asking you to believe anything on faith.

Q: How is this different from online astrology reports?

A: Online reports are generic and computer-generated. Your session is completely personalized, interactive, and combines multiple traditions for a unique perspective. Plus, you can ask questions and explore specific areas of interest.

Q: Can you predict my future?

A: Astrology shows trends, potentials, and timing, not fixed fate. I focus on empowering you to make informed decisions about your path rather than making predictions. You always have free will and choice in how you respond to cosmic influences.

Q: How far in advance should I book?

A: I recommend booking 5-7 days in advance to allow time for proper chart preparation. However, if you have urgent timing needs, reach out and we'll see what we can accommodate.

Q: Do you offer couples readings?

A: Yes! Compatibility readings for couples are one of my specialties. Both partners' birth information is needed, and you can choose to have the session together or separately.
