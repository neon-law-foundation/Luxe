Philosophy & Approach

"I believe that astrology is a tool for self-understanding and empowerment. By combining insights from different cultural perspectives, we can create a more complete picture of who you are and where you're going. Every reading is a conversation, and every chart tells a unique story."

Why Multiple Traditions?

Each astrological system offers different insights because they developed in different cultures with different goals and perspectives. Rather than seeing them as conflicting, I view them as complementary lenses that together create a more comprehensive understanding.

  • Western Astrology: Excels at psychological depth and modern life applications
  • Vedic Astrology: Provides spiritual and karmic understanding of your soul's journey
  • Chinese Astrology: Reveals elemental balance and natural timing wisdom
  • Integration: Together, they create a comprehensive view of your cosmic blueprint

My Reading Style:

Every session is interactive and conversational. I don't believe in one-way lectures or fatalistic predictions.
